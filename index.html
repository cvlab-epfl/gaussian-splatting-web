Usage:
Pick a point cloud (by default called point_cloud.ply, not input.ply).
Camera controls: W/S to move up/down image plane, A/D to move left/right, mouse wheel to move perpendicular. Mouse click+drag to rotate the camera (pitch/yaw).
If you have a cameras.json file, you can select a camera from the list to load it.
Over time (navigating) the camera may drift in terms of roll. Just reset to a predefined camera.
